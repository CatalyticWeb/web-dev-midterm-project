About Me

My name is Chase Harrison, and I am an aspiring Web Developer, coming from a background in Computer Science. A common challenge I hear people complain about in CS is the problem of practicality. Many CS developers can make things functional but, not usable. The opposite problem can be seen with many front end devs. They know how to make things look good, or seem usable, but sometimes getting a functional product requires a guiding hand for them. My skill set allows me to be grounded on both sides of the design fence. I understand both how to get a functional product, and also how to make it approachable for the average user.
